我们做什么What We Do

AESTRAT 是专注于中国医美行业的精品战略咨询机构。我们为国际品牌进入中国市场提供从市场研究、渠道策略到学术推广的全链路咨询服务，同时通过行业研究平台「研美思维」持续输出深度行业洞察。

AESTRAT is a boutique strategic advisory firm focused on China's aesthetic medicine industry. We provide international brands with end-to-end market entry services — from regulatory intelligence and channel strategy to KOL engagement and academic promotion — backed by deep industry research through our platform YanMei SiWei.

Since 2023 · Shanghai

为什么不同Why Different

创始人兼董事总经理 Giselle 拥有医学学位与商学院双重背景，历任国际医美企业中国区高管。我们不做泛泛而谈的咨询报告——每一个建议都基于对临床逻辑、监管环境和中国渠道生态的深度理解。

Our founder Giselle combines an MD from Shanghai Jiao Tong University with an EMBA from CEIBS, along with senior executive experience at Galderma, Innoaesthetics, and Meiwosi. Every recommendation we make is rooted in clinical logic, regulatory realities, and firsthand understanding of China's distribution ecosystem.
